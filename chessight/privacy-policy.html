Privacy Policy for Chessight

Last updated: April 13, 2026
Chessight does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

  • The app does not require user accounts or login.
  • Chess.com usernames entered by the user are used solely to fetch publicly available game data from the Chess.com public API.
  • No personal information is transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects anonymous crash reports and performance data to help us improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is included in crash reports.
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage statistics. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Chess.com Public API: Used to fetch publicly available player profiles and game data.

Data Storage

  • All data (saved profiles, cached analyses, engine settings) is stored locally on your device.
  • No data is uploaded to external servers.
